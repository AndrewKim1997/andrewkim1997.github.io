Biography

I am a machine learning researcher interested in developing trustworthy and interpretable machine learning methods. My research focuses on explainable AI, uncertainty quantification, machine learning fairness, AI governance, and AI ethics.

I am particularly interested in methods that help make model decisions more transparent, reliable, and accountable in high-stakes settings, where predictions should be not only accurate but also understandable, fair, and practically useful.
