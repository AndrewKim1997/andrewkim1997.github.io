Biography

I am a machine learning researcher interested in developing trustworthy and interpretable machine learning methods. My research focuses on explainable AI, algorithmic fairness, uncertainty quantification, and counterfactual explanation.

I am particularly interested in methods that help explain model decisions in high-stakes settings, where predictions should be not only accurate but also reliable, actionable, and understandable.
