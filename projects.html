Projects

Selected research projects in trustworthy machine learning, explainable AI, uncertainty quantification, fairness, governance, and AI ethics.

Sequential Counterfactual Explanation

A research project on generating actionable, stepwise counterfactual explanations under uncertain action dynamics.

Distributional Global Explanation

A project exploring distributional views of global feature importance, explanation stability, and subgroup-level heterogeneity.

Machine Learning Fairness and Error Burden

A project studying how erroneous model decisions impose different levels of individual burden, especially through counterfactual recourse and explanation multiplicity.
