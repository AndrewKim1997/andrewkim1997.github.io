Research

My research focuses on trustworthy machine learning, explainable AI, fairness, uncertainty, and counterfactual explanation.

Current Research Directions

Counterfactual Explanation under Uncertainty

This line of work studies how counterfactual explanations can become more actionable when model uncertainty, explanation multiplicity, and feasible feature changes are taken into account.

Trustworthy Machine Learning

This work investigates how machine learning systems can be made more interpretable, reliable, and fair in high-stakes decision settings.
